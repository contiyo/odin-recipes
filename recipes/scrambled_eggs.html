Scrambled Eggs by Akis

Description

These pita roll-ups are the perfect breakfast dish! Loaded with cheese, deli turkey, and creamy scrambled eggs, it's a recipe to add to your list of favorites!

Ingredient list

  • 6 eggs
  • 2 tablespoon(s) butter
  • 75g whole milk
  • salt
  • pepper
  • 2 pita breads, whole-wheat
  • 100g mozzarella, grated
  • 3 slices turkey
  • rocket
  • cherry tomatoes
  • olive oil

How to Make Scrambled Eggs Step-By-Step

  1. Put the eggs, salt, pepper, and milk into a bowl, and whisk them well until you have a smooth mixture.
  2. Place a frying pan over medium heat, add 1 tablespoon of butter, and allow it to melt but not burn.
  3. Add the egg mixture and stir with a silicone spatula for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Remove the pan from the heat, add the rest of the butter, and keep stirring.
  5. Arrange the pitas on your worktop, one next to the other, and divide the scrambled eggs and the mozzarella among them.
  6. Sprinkle with pepper and add 1 Â½ slices of deli turkey at the edge of each pita.
  7. Wrap the pitas into a roll, and then cut the roll into smaller pieces.
  8. Serve with rocket, cherry tomatoes, olive oil, and salt.
