Trahana Pie Recipe

Description

A hassle-free and speedy recipe for a comforting pie with phyllo and a rich filling with trahana pasta and cheese! You will only need 1 pan and 15 minutes to prepare it!

Ingredient list

  1. 125 g trahana pasta
  2. 4 phyllo dough sheets
  3. 500 g water
  4. 6-8 tablespoon(s) olive oil
  5. 125 g gruyere cheese, grated
  6. 0.5 tablespoon(s) oregano, dried

How to make trahana pie step by step

  1. Put the trahana pasta and the water into a heatproof bowl, and then cover the bowl with plastic wrap.
  2. Put the bowl in the microwave and cook the trahana pasta for 5 minutes at 800 Watts.
  3. Remove the bowl from the microwave and let it cool for 10 minutes.
  4. Take out the plastic wrap and stir the trahana with a spoon.
  5. Add the gruyere cheese and oregano, and mix them well.
  6. Add 1-2 tablespoons of olive oil to a 28 cm frying pan.
  7. Place the first phyllo sheet over half of the pan's surface, so that it is overhanging from the pan, and drizzle it with 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  8. Place 1 more phyllo sheet at the other half of the pan's surface, and drizzle it with another tablespoon of olive oil.
  9. Follow the same process for the other 2 phyllo sheets.
  10. Add the filling and spread it with a spoon.
  11. Fold the overhanging phyllo sheets inwards and drizzle them with the rest of the olive oil.
  12. Place the frying pan over medium-low heat, and cook the pie for 4-5 minutes.
  13. Turn the pie over, using a plate, and cook it for another 4-5 minutes until it is nicely golden and cooked through.
  14. Remove the pan from the heat and place the pie on a cutting board.
  15. Cut the trahana pie into 6 pieces and serve.
